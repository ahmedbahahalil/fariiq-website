Finance Overview is the landing page when clicking "Finance" in the main nav. It aggregates headline metrics from all finance submodules and links to each one.

Each submodule (Payroll, Cashflow, etc.) has its own summary as its landing page when you navigate to it. The Finance Overview doesn't duplicate those — it shows the one key metric per submodule to help you decide where to go.
